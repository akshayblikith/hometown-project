Top three activities to do at Bengaluru

Go for a picnic in the Cubbon Park

Nestled across 300 acres, Cubbon Park in the heart of Bangalore beckons as a captivating sightseeing destination, where lush green foliage thrives among more than 6,000 trees, nurturing a vibrant and diverse ecosystem.

Savor a diverse selection of dosa delights.

Bangalore and doses are a heavenly pair. The city of Bangalore tantalizes your taste buds with an unmatched variety of doses found nowhere else in the world. Imagine indulging in ghee-rich, spicy, and crispy doses served alongside flavorful chutney, aromatic sambhar, and delectable palya.

Get mesmerized by the Bengaluru Palace

The majestic Bengaluru Palace, spanning an impressive 45,000 square feet, seamlessly combines elements of Tudor and Scottish Gothic architecture, resulting in its grand and captivating presence.
